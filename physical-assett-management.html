Physical Asset Management

We are dedicated to support our clients’ Physical Asset Management needs in every stage of assets lifecycle.

Our support areas at high-level are:

  • Develop Asset management (AM) and Reliability frameworks
  • ISO 55001 gap analysis, GFMAM maturity assessment and audits
  • Strategic (SAMP) and tactical (TAMP) asset management plans
  • Portfolio-wide assets risk/cost profiling
  • Lifecycle costing, analysis and optimisation
  • Integrate emerging technologies in reliability, maintenance and AM
  • Asset disposal management

Physical Asset management is essential in delivering a sustainable, efficient and best value creating business. Done better, it helps organisations make well informed decisions, improve cost optimisation and control, demonstrate regulatory compliance, build stakeholder trust and manage risks.

JUFANDO delivers asset management services drawing our deep expertise, years of extensive experience and innovation.

Physical Asset Management involves coordinated, integrated and optimised planning, acquisition, operation, maintenance and disposal or renewal of assets, systems and portfolios.

We work in collaboration and along with your in-house teams and independently to achieve your best interest and results. We dedicate a fresh set of eyes to your issues and workup one of best ways to proceed forward. We take care of you concerns as ours.
