Maintenance Management

We are committed to support our clients’ Maintenance Management requirements while discovering their untapped potentials.

Our support areas at high-level are:

  • New CMMS implementation including training and documentation
  • Maintenance planning and scheduling advise - short to long term
  • Develop precision install/maintain procedures
  • Inventory, spares & rotables management
  • Zero based defensible maintenance budget develop and contract/contractor management framework
  • Maintenance staffing estimation and optimisation
  • TPM program, LEAN maintenance strategy implement and integrate S+5S practices in maintenance workflow, workshop & stores

Maintenance is the process of managing unreliability risk and all compliant to stakeholders of assets in an organisation.
JUFANDO delivers maintenance management services drawing our deep expertise, years of extensive experience and innovation.

As any built environment will deteriorate due to numerous stresses, usage and time; to preserve their current functionality, integrity and operational cost, they should be maintained using best practices.
We work in collaboration and along with your in-house teams and independently to achieve your best interest and results. We dedicate a fresh set of eyes to your issues and workup one of best ways to proceed forward. We take care of you concerns as ours.
