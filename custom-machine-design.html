Custom Machine Design & Mechanical Engineering

We are excited to undertake our clients’ Mechanical Engineering projects with advanced capabilities.

We bring resolutions to your concerns and imaginations in following areas:

  • Custom machines and products develop
  • Reverse engineering
  • 3D solid modelling, CAD technical/manufacturing/production drawing/drafting and bill of materials (BOM)
  • Plant-system-machine dynamic analysis, modifications and improvements (including rotating/reciprocating machinery)
  • Design for manufacture and assembly (DfMA) consulting and design failure modes and effects analysis (DFMEA) (for OEMs)
  • Mechanical systems feasibility studies, scope of work and work packages development for capital projects
  • Analyse, manage testing and certify structural integrity of mechanical equipment for standards and compliance (ISO & ANZS)
  • Ergonomic studies (design-production-maintenance contexts)
  • Compliant (rigid to flexible) and linkage mechanisms develop
  • Impeller, turbine and pressure vessel design
  • Structural FEA and motion simulation and optimisation
  • Advanced dynamic simulation (fatigue-thermal-buckle-frequency) and optimisation
  • Advanced drop test, CFD and DEM simulation and optimisation
  • G and NC coding for computer aided manufacturing (CAM) and robotics programming for production/operation

Mechanical Engineering is concerned with anything from “paper pin” to “space shuttle”. It involves anything considered as a machine or a machine part with a mass, motions, forces and energy transfer.
JUFANDO delivers mechanical engineering services drawing our deep expertise, years of extensive experience and innovation.
As an essential part of human advancement, Mechanical Engineering contributed to ancient societies and now has grown to deal with and resolve issues related to modern day global challenges in food, water, housing, poverty, safety and health, environment, energy, climate change, transportation and exploration of the universe.
We work in collaboration and along with your in-house teams and independently to achieve your best interest and results. We dedicate a group of fresh eyes to your issues and workup one of best ways to proceed forward. We take care of you concerns as ours.
