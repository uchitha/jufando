Reliability Engineering

We are thrilled to support our clients’ Reliability Engineering needs with world-class full spectrum reliability capabilities.

We bring solutions to your challenges in following areas:

  1. Reliability in system and product designs, validation and life testing (for OEMs)
  2. Operational readiness (full-scale program manage)
  3. Optimise planned maintenance (PMs) (using RCM/FMEA, PMO and engineering)
  4. Resonance prediction studies, prevention advice and experiment (for new & current installations)
  5. Hydrocarbon (HC) & lubrication consulting (full-scale HC program)
  6. Asset integrity program (regulatory assets & classified plants)
  7. Corrosion prediction, prevention and control
  8. Predictive maintenance and condition monitoring program develop, failure diagnostics, prognostics and remaining useful life (RUL) estimate
  9. Optimal component & asset replacement, inspections & condition monitoring and maintenance resources modelling
  10. Develop reliability KPIs and “Data Mining” from CMMS & downtime reports to improve performance-cost-risk balance
  11. Application/equipment performance review, failure analysis & RCA; process loss accounting/analysis and defect elimination
  12. Warranty models and planning (for OEMs)
  13. New OEE (overall equipment effectiveness) system implementation & enhancement

Reliability engineering strategically improves assets throughout their life cycle and manages business risks by designing reliable systems, redesign and modification of assets, preventing failures, losses and elimination of defects.
JUFANDO delivers reliability engineering services drawing our deep expertise, years of extensive experience and innovation.
Reliability engineering supports every stage of asset lifecycle from design to dispose to achieve optimum reliability and minimum lifecycle cost and risk for businesses.
We work in collaboration and along with your in-house teams and independently to achieve your best interest and results. We dedicate a fresh group of eyes to your issues and workup one of best ways to proceed forward. We take care of you concerns as ours.
