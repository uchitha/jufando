Injection Mould Design

  1. Home
  2. Injection Mould Design

JUFANDO innovates injection moulds and components to achieve client’s specific requirements and “design for manufacture, production and cost effectiveness” in mind.

Developing a customised and fit-for-purpose plastic product is an inventive process. This process needs to consider, special requirements such as plastic material injected, precession required, integrity, and finally injection mould-ability on top of efficient moulding cycle time for high-volume production and cost of mould manufacture.

We design such products drawing our deep engineering expertise, years of extensive experience and innovation.

Mould design is one of the most complex design processes. Not only are the part geometries challenging, but the bad imported part features, missing drafts, impossible shut-offs and undercuts, weld lines, gate placement, venting, runner system, cooling & heating, ejecting, core metal and base as well as number of cavities can all add time and cost to mould design.

JUFANDO can bring your ideas to reality in areas of:

  1. Generic moulds and tools design / development
  2. Speciality moulds and tools design / development (stripper/3-plate, hydraulic-pneumatic, gear driven, angular sliders, etc.)
  3. Auxiliary jigs and fixtures design
  4. EDM electrodes design
  5. 3D motion and melt flow analysis
  6. 2D/3D models, shop/manufacturing/production drawings
  7. G and NC codes for manufacturing
